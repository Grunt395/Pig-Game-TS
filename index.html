HOW TO PLAY:

  • Two Players are needed to play this game.
  • Players can click the "Roll Dice" button to roll the dice (1-6).
  • The current score below a player will increment with each successive dice roll.
  • If a player rolls a 1, their turn will abruptly end, and any accrued points will be lost.
  • Click the "Hold" button to save the current score and rack up points!
  • Upon holding a score, the current turn will end.
  • The first player to "Hold" 100 points is the winner!
  • Click the "New Game" button to reset the game!

    • (Tip: Players can press the SPACE key to roll the dice or the ENTER key to hold their score!)
